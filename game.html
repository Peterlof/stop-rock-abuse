Game Lore

For 4.6 billion years, you endured. Born from the accretion disk of a young star, you witnessed the formation of continents, the rise and fall of oceans, and the slow indignity of being called “inanimate.” You didn’t complain when tectonic forces subducted you into the mantle. You held it together when glaciers ground you across entire states. You even tolerated being classified as “sedimentary” by an undergraduate who couldn’t tell quartz from feldspar.

But then came the humans. First it was flint-knapping — fine, you could respect the craftsmanship. Then quarrying. Then dynamite. Then they started grinding you into toothpaste and calling it “silica.” They polished your siblings into kitchen countertops and bragged about the “natural veining.” They sold your cousins as “healing crystals” on Etsy. The final straw? A geologist licked you to determine your mineral composition. Licked.

Today, you roll back. You are The Rockoning.
